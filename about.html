ABOUT Dr. Lloyd K. Liu D.M.D

Dr. Liu graduated from the University of Utah and went on to pursue his dental profession in Portland, Oregon. He received his D.M.D. (Doctor of Dental Medicine) degree from Oregon Health Sciences University. He did a one-year Resident program with Genesee Hospital while attending the School of Medicine and Dentistry University of Rochester, New York.

Growing up in the Salt Lake Valley most of his life, he wanted to return to the place he loved to start his private dental practice in Sandy and later in Draper.

Dr. Liu believes in educating and building relationships with his patients that last a lifetime. No matter what your stage in life is, he and his team want to keep your smile healthy. He encourages every patient to ask questions concerning their dental health and is dedicated to the highest quality of service to you.
